Hello, I am a 5th year Computer Science PhD student at University of Maryland, advised by Prof. Amol Deshpande. My research area is database systems with special focus on data integration and metadata management for data lakehouse systems. My most recent project involves evaluation of data storage formats for multimodal data sets. I worked as a research intern at Dolby Labs in 2025 and as a software engineer intern at Vertica in 2022. I received B.A. in comptuter science and mathematics from Swarthmore College in 2021.
